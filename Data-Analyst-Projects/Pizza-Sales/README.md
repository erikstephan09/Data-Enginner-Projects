\## üìù Project Summary



This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover insights on revenue performance, customer purchase behavior, and product popularity. Using \*\*SQL\*\* for data extraction, cleaning, and aggregation, and \*\*Power BI\*\* for dashboard creation, the project delivers a complete analytical overview of business performance.



Key KPIs included:

\- \*\*Total Revenue\*\*

\- \*\*Total Orders\*\*

\- \*\*Total Pizzas Sold\*\*

\- \*\*Average Order Value (AOV)\*\*

\- \*\*Average Pizzas per Order\*\*



The dashboard also reveals:

\- Daily and monthly order trends  

\- Customer preferences by pizza size and category  

\- Top- and bottom-performing pizza products by revenue, quantity sold, and order count  



By integrating structured SQL models with dynamic Power BI visualizations, this project provides actionable insights to help optimize operations, improve menu strategy, and enhance overall sales performance.



---



\## üìå Business Problem



The company lacked a consolidated analytical view of its pizza sales data, making it difficult to:



\- Track crucial sales KPIs  

\- Identify daily and monthly trends for staffing and inventory planning  

\- Understand customer preferences across pizza categories and sizes  

\- Detect top and low-performing pizzas to guide product and marketing strategy  



Without these insights, decision-making was fragmented and heavily intuition-based.



This project solves that gap by building a complete analytics dashboard that centralizes KPIs, trends, and product-level performance to support data-driven decisions.
